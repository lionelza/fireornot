Fire or Not — Child Safety Standards

Effective date: April 2026  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Fire or Not is an adults-only app intended exclusively for users aged 18 and over. The protection of minors is a non-negotiable commitment. This page describes the standards and measures in place to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in connection with our app.
01

Our commitment

Fabbli, the developer of Fire or Not, has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. We are committed to ensuring that our app cannot be used to produce, distribute, or facilitate CSAE material or grooming behaviour.

Fire or Not is designed exclusively as a private fantasy game for adult couples. It is not a social network, a chat platform, or a discovery service. The design of the app inherently limits the risk of misuse.

02

Age restriction and verification

Fire or Not is rated for adults only (18+) and is published in restricted content categories on Google Play. The following measures are in place to prevent minors from accessing the app:

  • Age gate at first launch — Every new user must actively confirm they are 18 or older before accessing any content. The app cannot be used without this confirmation.
  • Store-level age restrictions — The app is listed under mature content categories on Google Play, which applies platform-level age restrictions.
  • No content accessible without confirmation — No questions, matches, or features are visible before the age confirmation step.
03

App design safeguards

The architecture of Fire or Not significantly reduces the risk of CSAE by design:

  • No public profiles — Users have no public-facing profile, username, photo, or bio. There is no way to discover or browse other users.
  • No stranger interaction — The app is strictly a two-player experience. Users can only connect with one specific partner using a private 6-digit code shared directly between them. There is no ability to interact with unknown users.
  • No image or video sharing — The app does not support the uploading, sharing, or viewing of any images or videos.
  • No public chat — There is no messaging, chat, or comment feature. The only user-generated content is short private text (up to 150 characters) shared exclusively between the two paired partners.
  • No user discovery — There are no feeds, search functions, or ways to find other users in the app.
Because of this design, Fire or Not cannot be used as a platform to contact, solicit, or exploit minors in any way.
04

Content standards

All question content in the app is written and reviewed by the developer before publication. No user-generated content is publicly surfaced. The following standards apply to all content:

  • All content involves only consenting adults in legal adult scenarios.
  • No content depicts, references, or implies minors in any sexual or inappropriate context.
  • Custom questions submitted by users (wildcards) are stored privately and are only shared between the two paired partners. They are subject to the same content prohibitions.
05

Reporting and response

If you believe this app has been used in connection with CSAE activity, or if you have concerns about child safety in relation to this app, please contact us immediately:

06

Updates to these standards

These child safety standards will be reviewed and updated as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We are committed to maintaining and improving our child safety practices over time.
