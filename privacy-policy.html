Fire or Not — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Fire or Not is built on a simple promise: your private answers stay private. This policy explains exactly what data is collected, why, and how it is protected. We have deliberately kept data collection to the minimum necessary for the app to function.
01

Who we are

Fire or Not is an independent app published by Fabbli, an individual developer. For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable privacy laws, Fabbli acts as the data controller for personal data processed through this application.

Contact: info@fireornot.app

02

What data we collect and why

Fire or Not does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personally identifying information. The following data is collected automatically when you use the app:

  • Anonymous user identifier (UID) — Generated automatically by Firebase on first launch. This is a random string with no connection to your identity. It is used to link your votes and matches to your session.
  • 6-character pairing code — A short alphanumeric code derived from your UID, used so you and your partner can connect within the app. Stored in our database and visible only to you.
  • Vote and match data — Your YES, NO, and MAYBE swipes are stored to calculate matches with your partner. NO votes are never shared with your partner and are only used to determine when no match exists.
  • Wildcard questions — Any custom fantasy text you write (up to 150 characters) is stored and shared only with your paired partner.
  • Push notification token — If you grant notification permission, your device token is stored to send match alerts. You can revoke this at any time in your device settings.
  • Device language preference — Read on first launch to set the app language. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Anonymous deck interest analytics — When you tap on a question deck, a counter is incremented in our database with no link to your identity. Used only to understand which content is most popular.
We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, payment information, or any other personally identifying information.
03

Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests — Processing the anonymous UID, pairing code, votes, and match data is necessary to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Consent — Push notification tokens are only processed if you explicitly grant notification permission on your device.
04

How data is stored and protected

All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service operated by Google LLC. Firebase applies encryption at rest and in transit. Data is stored in Google Cloud infrastructure, which may be located outside your country of residence.

Access to your data is restricted by Firestore security rules: only you and your paired partner can read your shared match data. No other users can access your information.

For more information on Google's data practices, see the Firebase Privacy and Security page.

05

Data sharing and third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes. The only third-party service that processes your data is:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication) — Database and anonymous authentication provider.
  • Expo Push Notification Service — Used to deliver match notifications to your device if you have granted permission.

Both services are used solely to operate the app and are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

06

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists in the app. You can permanently delete all your data at any time using the Delete Account option in the app's Settings tab. This will cascade-delete all associated Firestore records including votes, matches, and your pairing code, and create a fresh anonymous session.

After deletion, no data associated with your previous session is retained in our database.

07

Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — You can delete all your data at any time via the app, or by contacting us.
  • Right to restriction — You can ask us to restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to object — You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to data portability — You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@fireornot.app. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's privacy

Fire or Not is intended exclusively for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has used the app, please contact us immediately and we will delete the associated data.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
