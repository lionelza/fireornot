Fire or Not — Data Deletion

Last updated: March 2026

You are in full control of your data. Fire or Not makes it easy to permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app — no email required, no waiting period.
01

How to delete your account

Follow these steps inside the app:

  1. Open the Fire or Not app
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom of the screen)
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings page
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm when prompted — deletion is immediate and permanent
Deletion is instant. Once confirmed, your data cannot be recovered.
02

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following from our systems:

  • Your anonymous account — your Firebase identity is deleted immediately
  • Your pairing code — the 6-character code used to connect with your partner
  • All your votes — every YES, NO, and MAYBE swipe you have made
  • All matches — any questions you and your partner both said yes to
  • Your couple connection — the link between you and your partner is dissolved
  • Any Wildcard questions — custom questions you created are permanently removed
  • Your push notification token — you will no longer receive match notifications
  • All local app data — everything stored on your device by the app is wiped

After deletion, the app automatically creates a fresh anonymous session so you can start over if you wish.

03

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to access the app and would like your data deleted, you can contact us directly and we will handle the deletion manually within 30 days.
